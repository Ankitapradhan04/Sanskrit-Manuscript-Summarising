How a manuscript gets here

01 · Ingest

OCR + quality filter

Every page is scored on OCR confidence, Devanagari ratio, and garbage-token rate before it's trusted.

02 · Reference

Vocabulary + grammar

A hand-verified starter dictionary, a full Monier-Williams import, and patterns mined from good manuscripts.

03 · Translate

Gloss, then polish

Exact → stemmed → sandhi → fuzzy matching, each tagged with its confidence, then optionally smoothed into fluent English.
